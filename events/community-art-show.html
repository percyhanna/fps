Community Art Show

Call for Submissions — Open to the Public!

The Foothills Philharmonic Chorus invites the public to display art inspired by the concert theme “We Can Mend the Sky: Songs of Hope”

All Art and all Ages are welcome!

All Artists who submit will receive a complimentary ticket to one of the concerts.

Details

  • April 1 — Email your intent to participate to marketing@footphil.com. Please include a short description of your art with Title, materials, and approximate size.
  • May 5 — Art must be delivered to Okotoks Alliance Church.
  • May 9 — Art must be picked up in the evening after the concert.

*Artists are responsible for display materials (easel, frame, table stand, etc.)

Concert Information

Fri. May 8, 2026 — 7:30 PM
Sat. May 9, 2026 — 3:00 PM
Location: Okotoks Alliance Church

With Community Art Show & Special Guest Performance from Alberta Dance Academy.

Tickets: $20
Students and Seniors: $15
Children 12 and under: FREE
Available at footphil.com or at the door.
