Friends With Tunes

Men’s Chorus & Vocal Jazz

Spring is in the air and as the flowers and birds return, so do two of our ensembles! The Foothills Philharmonic Men's Chorus and FP Vocal Jazz combine forces to bring you music to lighten your heart and lift your spirits! Featuring special guest performers Cadence Creek, we bring you a night featuring a little bit of everything - sacred, secular, jazz, The Beatles, country, bluegrass, and more! Join us for a night of uplifting music from across the ages, featuring musicians from across the Foothills Region!

May 6th, 7:00pm
Okotoks Alliance Church
