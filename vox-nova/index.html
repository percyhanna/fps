Vox Nova was created in the Summer of 2023, under the direction of Sarah McLean. The purpose of Vox Nova is to provide professional and/or experienced vocalists from the Foothills area the opportunity to perform complex repertoire to a high standard. This ensemble will have minimal rehearsal sessions; once music is handed out, musicians will be expected to learn their parts at home before coming together with the rest of the group a few days before the performance.
