Foothills Philharmonic Junior Singers

Join the Foothills Philharmonic Junior Singers for their inaugural season! Rehearsals begin January 18th, 2024.

The Junior Singers will be split up into two age groups:

Prelude
  • Ages 6-7 as of Dec 31, 2023
  • Rehearsals on Thursdays from 5:30-6:30pm
Cantabile
  • Ages 8-12 as of Dec 31, 2023
  • Rehearsals on Thursdays from 6:45-8pm

Rehearsals being held at Grace Lutheran Church

If you have any questions about the Junior Singers, please email us at juniorsingers@footphil.com

  • Regular: $200

Registration

All musicians participating in one of the Foothills Philharmonic ensembles must first register to become a member of the Foothills Philharmonic Society. Once you have registered as a member of the Society, you can register for one or more of our musical ensembles.

Note: Each musician must register individually as a Member of the Society. You will need a unique email address for each member. For children/youth, an adult should register on behalf of their child (using the child's name), but use an email address from one of the parents or guardians for communication purposes.

Below, you will find links to these two steps required for joining the Junior Singers:

  1. Become a Member of the Society

    Click below to register your child as a Member of the Foothills Philharmonic Society. Please use an email address of a parent or guardian while registering.

  2. Register for the Junior Singers

    After registering as a Member of the Society, you can join the Junior Singers by clicking the following link:
