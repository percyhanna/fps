Heather was raised in a musical family and church community and at a very young age started playing for church and working with the choirs and preparing concerts. This was the start of her love of accompanying and teaching music.

For 25 plus years she has had a busy music studio teaching piano, voice and theory. Composing her own music resulted in her cd of 15 original songs in 2009. She has been accompanying and arranging music for the Foothills Mens Chorus since their start and for 13 years she accompanied and directed the Calgary Protestant Male Choir.

She is very happy to be the Musical Director for the Foothills Philharmonic Vocal Jazz and Men’s Chorus!
