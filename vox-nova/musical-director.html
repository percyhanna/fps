Vox Nova was created in the Summer of 2023, under the direction of Sarah McLean and Tim Korthuis. Vox Nova exists to provide professional and/or experienced vocalists from the Foothills area the opportunity to perform complex repertoire to a high standard, with minimal group rehearsal commitment.

When a performance is booked, the repertoire will be handed out one month in advance, and musicians will be expected to learn their parts at home before coming together for full ensemble rehearsals a few days before the performance.
