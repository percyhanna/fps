Music To Colour Your Fall

Music To Colour Your Fall

Orchestra & Chamber Chorus

The Foothills Philharmonic Orchestra and Chamber Chorus open the concert season by teaming up this October! Featuring the works of Beethoven, Schumann, Whitacre, and more!

The leaves are turning, the air is getting chilly, and the Foothills Philharmonic is back! For one night only, our Orchestra and Chamber chorus come together in an evening that features the works of Beethoven, Schumann, Whitacre, and more! Whether your autumn puts you in a romantic mood, as with Whitacre's "Five Hebrew Love Songs" or more meditative, like Beethoven's "Elegischer Gesang", we have something for everyone's fall mood as we bring you a night of music that will have you envisioning the leaves crunching underfoot and the warmth of that favourite sweater. Tickets available at the door or online.

Tickets:
  • Adults: $20
  • Students and Seniors: $15
  • Children 12 and under: Free
  • Pre-purchase online, or at the door
