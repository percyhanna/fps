The Youth Strings is our newest ensemble that started in the spring of 2023. This ensemble is for students aged 5 to 18, and we host two separate groups: a beginner group and an intermediate group. Students will learn to play with others, making beautiful music together, as well as participate in Master Classes, sectional and leadership opportunities. We will tackle music from a variety of genres, including traditional baroque, popular and movie music, as well as fiddle/folk tunes.

The Youth Strings ensemble is directed by Madeline Bailey and Karen Dees.
