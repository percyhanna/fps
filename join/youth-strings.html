Details

Chamber music education for students, aged 5-18! We will be hosting a BEGINNER group for young musicians who have just started to learn their instruments (up to RCM level 2) and an INTERMEDIATE group for those who are approximately RCM level 3-5. Students will learn to play with others, making beautiful music together, as well as participate in Master Classes, sectional and leadership opportunities. We will tackle music from a variety of genres, including traditional baroque, popular and movie music, as well as fiddle/folk tunes. Concert date is scheduled for Sunday, May 28 - matinee. Directors: Madeline Bailey and Karen Dees
