Sounds Like Spring

Foothills Philharmonic Orchestra

The Foothills Philharmonic Orchestra will be performing in their first performances, featuring guest soloist Mr. Stuart Lloyd with Michèle Wheatley-Brown.

Featured music includes Franz Schubert’s Trout Quintet, our flute trio performing Dance of the Reeds from Tchaichovsky’s Nutcracker Suite, and our brass quartet bringing Giles Farnaby’s Fancies, Toyes and Dreams.
