Sounds Like Spring

Orchestra

Join the Foothills Philharmonic Orchestra for an evening that sounds like spring. If Pinot Grigio was music rather than a light white wine, it would sound like this! We’ll be featuring Ursula Sherwood as soloist on the Carl Stamitz flute concerto, plus presenting George Butterworth’s “The Banks of Green Willow” and Edward Elgar’s “Enigma - theme and variations”.

June 15th, 8:00pm
Okotoks Alliance Church
