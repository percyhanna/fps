Upcoming Concerts

10th Anniversary Concert

Foothills Philharmonic Society

Ten years ago, we began with a single choir. Now, a whole decade later, we invite you to celebrate the Society's tenth anniversary season with our many groups! Featuring a silent auction, performances of some of our favourite music from the last ten years and the debut of the our newest group, the Junior Singers, we can't wait to celebrate the Society's first decade with you!

Children 12 and under are free, but they require a ticket.

Online ticket sales will end at midnight the night before the concert. Tickets are available at the door.

February 25th, 3:00pm
Okotoks Alliance Church
