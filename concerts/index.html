Upcoming Concerts

FPS Goes to the Movies

Chorus & Orchestra

Music brings the stories we tell to life! And so many of the movies we have loved are animated by the music of orchestras and choirs. We’ve picked some of the greats, and hope you’ll join us in celebrating the music of modern story telling! Featuring both original selections from films such as Star Wars and The Lord of the Rings, and well-loved classical pieces from major films including Orff's “O Fortuna” and Mozart's “Requiem”, we invite you to experience music from timeless tales and bask in the stories we all love to tell and retell. Bring your family and your imagination, and relive some great movie moments with us!

Spring Strings

Youth Strings

We are very pleased to present our very first performance of our Foothills Youth String Orchestra, featuring an exciting blend of classical, pop and movie music! The beginner ensemble will kick off the concert with a beautiful rendition of Holst’s “Jupiter” from The Planets, a lively and engaging performance of “Drunken Sailor” that is sure to get the audience's feet tapping, as well as a wonderful energetic and gritty piece titled “Pirates of Aegan” and more.

The Intermediates have diligently prepared Vivaldi’s “Concerto for Strings in D major, RV121” which will highlight the precision and musicianship of our young musicians as they perform its intricate melodies and beautiful harmonies. Showcasing their versatility next, they will switch gears into some pop music, followed by movie music including Top Gun, and Mission Impossible, and much more!

May 28th, 3:00pm
Okotoks Alliance Church
