The FP Vocal Jazz originated as an idea two members of the chorus who wanted to explore jazz choral music. After a wonderful successful trial in the summer of 2016, their first official season started in the fall 2016, with their first concert being fall of 2016 performing to a near sold out show.

Since then it has become Ladies Vocal Jazz, a smaller group selected by audition, performing more difficult repertoire. Recent performances (outside of their concerts), include Picnic in the Park, and the Okotoks Canada Day Celebration.

They love singing for the seniors homes in the Foothills area for the Christmas and Spring seasons, and their concerts are regularly combined with the FP Men's Chorus, with a special guest from the Foothills area.

They are excited to share their music in the coming season, including their upcoming Christmas concert, featuring Christmas songs from well loved Christmas movies.

* The Ladies Vocal Jazz is an auditioned FPS Ensemble.
