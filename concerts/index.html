Upcoming Concerts

10th Anniversary Concert

Foothills Philharmonic Society

Ten years ago, we began with a single choir. Now, a whole decade later, we invite you to celebrate the Society's tenth anniversary season with our many groups! Featuring performances of some of our favourite music from the last ten years, and the debut of the our newest group, the Junior Singers, we can't wait to celebrate the Society's first decade with you!

Children 12 and under are free, but they require a ticket.

Online ticket sales will end at midnight the night before the concert. Tickets are available at the door.

February 25th, 3:00pm
Okotoks Alliance Church

The Canadian Chamber Choir

The Canadian Chamber Choir with the Foothills Philharmonic Chamber Chorus

Experience the JUNO-nominated Canadian Chamber Choir live in concert during their first-ever visit to Okotoks, and their first return to the Calgary Metropolitan Region since 2007.

Currently touring a program featuring the works of Canadian composers Hussein Janmohamed, Sherryl Sewepagaham, Mari Alice Conrad, Tracy Wong and more, the CCC has even been known to break out some Gordon Lightfoot or Joni Mitchell on occasion!

February 24th, 7:30pm
Okotoks Alliance Church

Buy Tickets

Vocal Spa Day

Julia Davids and the Canadian Chamber Choir

Join individual singers and choristers from Okotoks and the surrounding area for a Vocal Spa Day. Dr. Julia Davids and members of the Canadian Chamber Choir are here to help you get back into peak condition with helpful warm-ups, SOVT exercises, exploration of flexible sound, and reminders of good technique. Participants will also learn a piece, culminating in a performance at that evening's 7:30 PM concert. (The concert is free for workshop participants!)

February 24th, 3-5pm
Okotoks Alliance Church
