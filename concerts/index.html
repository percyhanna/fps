Bach Christmas Oratorio

Orchestra & Chorus

Selections from Bach's Christmas Oratorio and other Christmas favourites.

Come celebrate the season with Bach’s well loved Christmas Oratorio, performed by the Foothills Philharmonic‘s Chorus and Orchestra. You will enjoy beautiful selections from this work and other favourites sure to get you in the Christmas mood, including Sleigh Ride, and beautiful carol arrangements.

Children 12 and under are free, but they require a ticket.

Online ticket sales will end at midnight the night before the concert. Tickets are available at the door.
