Many works are composed specifically for ensembles with smaller numbers, while many larger works are adapted for these chamber choruses as well. The Foothills Philharmonic Chamber Chorus is an auditioned group that loves performing these chamber works, and has gained a reputation for performing difficult works of high quality.

The Chamber Chorus is an auditioned FPS Ensemble.
