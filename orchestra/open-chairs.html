Join the Orchestra

We’re currently welcoming new members! The Foothills Philharmonic Orchestra is made up of musicians from the Foothills area (Okotoks, High River, south Calgary, Dewinton, Diamond Valley, etc). We have openings for violins, cellos, double bass, and most brass instruments.

Rehearsals are held Wednesdays at 7:30 PM in Okotoks.

If you’re interested in playing with us, we’d love to hear from you.

Email orchestra@footphil.com for more information.
